Minghua He

I am currently a graduate student at Peking University, the National Engineering Research Center for Software Engineering. I've also had great experiences working at Tencent, Microsoft Research Asia (MSRA) and Alibaba Group. My research interest is broadly in Foundational LLMs and Reliable AI/Systems. I am dedicated to bridging the gap between AI and systems in the real world, and making AI and systems more reliable.
